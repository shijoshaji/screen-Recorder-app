## ğŸ® Usage

1. **Start Recording**:
   - Click the "Start Recording" button or press `Ctrl + Alt + R`
   - Select the screen/window you want to record
   - Optionally enable audio capture

2. **Stop Recording**:
   - Click the "Stop Recording" button or press `Ctrl + Alt + R` again
   - Your recording will appear in the preview section

3. **Download**:
   - Click "Download Video" or press `Ctrl + Alt + D`
   - The video will be saved as a WebM file
